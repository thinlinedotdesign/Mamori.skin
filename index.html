02 · The Science, Briefly

Light, at the right
wavelength,
does real work.

The science is older than the trend. NASA studied LED light for tissue repair in the 1990s. The difference between a device that works and one that merely glows is measurable.

633
Red Light
633–660 nm

Stimulates fibroblasts at depth, supporting collagen and elastin renewal. The wavelength range behind most credible anti-ageing claims.

Collagen Fine Lines Tone
830
Near-Infrared
830–850 nm

Penetrates deeper than visible light, reaching tissue layers that respond with reduced inflammation, accelerated recovery, and better barrier function.

Depth Inflammation Recovery
415
Blue Light
415–470 nm

Targets surface bacteria responsible for inflammatory acne — a precise, drug-free intervention used alongside red and NIR.

Acne Bacteria Clarity

A wavelength accurate to within ±5 nm of target. An irradiance of 30–70 mW/cm². Medical-grade LEDs. A device measured by a spectrometer, not just photographed for an ad. We will publish ours.

03 · The Mamori Standard

What we won't ship.

Each item disqualifies a device from carrying our name, regardless of margin, lead time, or how good it looks in a photograph.

01
No cosmetic LEDs.

Only medical-grade emitters with verified wavelength accuracy. Spectrometer reports go on the box, not in the marketing.

02
No vague claims.

If we can't measure it, we don't say it. Irradiance, exposure time, wavelength tolerance — all disclosed, all the time.

03
No shortcut certifications.

BIS for India. CE Medical. FDA-registered manufacturing. RoHS & EMC. The cost is what it is; the certificate is real.

04
No quiet recalls.

A serial number on every unit, traceable to its production lot. If we ever get something wrong, you'll hear from us first.
